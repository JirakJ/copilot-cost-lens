# Your AI spend, at a glance

The dashboard shows this month's **spend**, your **Copilot allowance gauge**, an **end-of-month forecast** and cost broken down **per repository, model and day** — with charts, an activity heatmap and your most expensive sessions.

Open it from the activity bar (graph icon) or run **Copilot Cost Lens: Open Dashboard**.

Everything is read from local log files. Nothing leaves your machine.
