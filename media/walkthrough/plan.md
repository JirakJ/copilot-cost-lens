# Match the gauge to your plan

Pick your Copilot plan in **Settings → Copilot Cost Lens → Plan**:

- **Business** (300 premium requests ≈ 1,900 AI credits)
- **Enterprise** (1,000 premium requests)
- promo variants, or **Custom** with your own credit allowance

The allowance gauge then shows how much of your included credits you have used — and when they are forecast to run out.

You can also set a **monthly USD budget** and get warned at a configurable percentage.
